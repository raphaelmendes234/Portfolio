Jeu de societe

I made a game ! It was a team project where we imagined the theme, the rules and the type of the game.


It is a board game about Egypt, where you play as a team and try to get out of the pyramid. But be careful of the traps !


This work allowed me to learn more about the conception of a game, the realisation, the illustration and building the diffrents parts of the game.
