The Weeknd vinyl cover

This work is about a vinyl cover for The Weeknd's album DawnFM.


I've composed it with elements that stick to the theme of the album and tried to give a shining tone and so link it to the title of the album. Creating a whole design that had to stick to a theme and atmosphere was really inspiring.


Thanks to this work i learnt more about Photoshop while working with the face and creating the light effects and Illustrator for all the fonts and logo. I was really happy to work with two applications and bind them together to create this cool vinyl !
